Horace Mann Covid Aid Club

Corona Pandemic Response

The Covid Aid Club's mission is to help the Horace Mann community, and those who live in it through this pandemic Our first act as a club will be to target small bussinesses near Horace Mann. We know all the fond memories Horace Mann students, including ourselves, have had at these establishments, whether it be doing your homework with a hot slice of pizza, or enjoying a burger with friends. We would like to sanitize these neighboring restaurants to prevent against covid, and specifically ask the owners what they would need during this taxing time. We will also run a survey directed at the general public to guage what their needs might be.

Contents of this site

On this site find information about the symptons of Corona, what we know so far about the virus, and what we will do to help you during this pandemic! If each and every one of us take action to keep ourselves safe from the virus, it will help on a nationwide scale!
