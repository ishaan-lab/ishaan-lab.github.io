Symptoms

According to the CDC, people with COVID-19 have the following symptoms:

  • coughing
  • fever
  • shortness of breath
  • chills
  • shaking
  • muscle pain
  • headache
  • sore throat
  • loss of taste or smell

You will exhibit these symptoms around 2-14 days after being infected by the virus.
