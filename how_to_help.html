How We Will Help

How We Will Help!

    We will be going into the neighborhood surrounding Horace Mann, and will be running surveys for both the small businesses, and the individuals. To start, we will be going to businesses like Broadway Joe's, Lloyd's Carrot Cake, and the many delis in the area. We will ask them if they would need any help during the Pandemic, and will be aiding them after school for about an hour. After contacting these small bussinesses, we will then focus on people living in the area. We will survey people coming out of the subway, lining up for busses, and possibly go door to door, asking what people might need during Covid19. You can preview this survey, and take it yourself, on this website under the 'survey' section. Please check that out before leaving the site.

Even if it makes just a tiny difference, we want to do anything we can to help our community!
